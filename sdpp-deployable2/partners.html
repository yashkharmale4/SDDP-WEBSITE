Meet our Partners

S.D.Dhanawate

Advocate Sakharam Dattatray Dhanawate is a senior legal professional with over four decades of experience, having commenced his practice in 1982. He has built a distinguished career with extensive courtroom experience at the Pune District & Sessions Court and the Bombay High Court.

His expertise spans Criminal law, Civil litigation, Family disputes, and Labour matters, where he has represented clients in a wide range of complex cases. Known for his strong legal acumen and practical approach, he focuses on delivering effective legal strategies and reliable solutions.

He has also served as a member of the Pune Bar Association and has been a practicing Notary since 1994, offering trusted and authorized documentation services. With a commitment to integrity and professional excellence, he continues to uphold the highest standards of legal practice.

N. S. Dhanwate

Adv. Nirajkumar S. Dhanawate is a Partner and Advocate with over a decade of legal practice, holding an LL.B. from DES Law College, Fergusson College Campus, Pune (Savitribai Phule Pune University , 2013). He regularly represents clients in Family law, Criminal matters, Civil suits, DRT proceedings, and Banking and Finance disputes before various courts and tribunals.

His practice focus is a practical and detail-oriented approach, with a strong focus on legal strategy, precise documentation, and procedural compliance. His practice combines both litigation and advisory services, enabling clients to navigate complex legal challenges effectively.

With a commitment to professionalism and client-centric service, he strives to deliver reliable, efficient, and result-oriented legal solutions. His practice includes an analytical approach, strategic thinking, and ability to handle complex legal matters with clarity and precision.
